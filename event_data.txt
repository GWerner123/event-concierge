EVENT: 2026 Tulsa Riverfront Music & Arts Expo
DATE: Saturday, June 20, 2026
HOURS: 10:00 AM – 11:00 PM
LOCATION: Tulsa River Parks (Gathering Place area)

MAIN STAGE SCHEDULE:
- 12:00 PM: Blue River Soul (Jazz/Blues)
- 3:00 PM: The Tulsa Collective (Indie Folk)
- 7:00 PM: Oklahoma Sound Orchestra (Headliner)
- 9:30 PM: Fireworks Finale

VENDOR INFO:
- Food Trucks: Located at the East Lawn (tacos, BBQ, vegan options)
- Art Market: Over 50 local artisans on the main walkway
- Beer Garden: Near the Boathouse (ID required)

LOGISTICS & TIPS:
- PARKING: Free shuttle from the OSU-Tulsa parking lot every 15 minutes. 
- PROHIBITED: No glass containers, no weapons, no outside alcohol.
- PETS: Dogs on leashes are welcome in the park areas.
- ADA: Accessible viewing area available at the front right of the Main Stage.
- WEATHER: Rain or shine. Bring sunscreen and a refillable water bottle.
